    Aaply Figma Plugin
    Export in a few clicks ↓
    Now that your amazing app are done, what's next? You can easily export it to Figma 🎉

    Components will be exported to the style guide, where all elements are arranged into buttons, checkboxes, etc.
    1
    Design your app in Aaply
    2
    Click on the export button
    3
    Follow the link to the Aaply Figma Plugin (project link was automatically copied to the clipboard)
    4
    Run it and simply paste the project link
    5
    Enjoy your mobile app wireframes in Figma
    Everything is ready to continue working on the mobile app UI. As simple as that!
